Mini Project Guides

 - The  goal of this mini-project is to write a short 6-page paper on a small research idea, which can hopefully be extended to a full paper later
 - The mini-project can be conducted personally or as a team of two
 - As the first step, please write a 2-page proposal
 - The proposal should include the following:
	 - Motivation
	 - Research problems (on any topic related to this course, e.g., mobile sensing, efficient ml models and systems)
	 - Existing solutions and their limitations
	 - The approach to tackle the problem
	 - Expected technical challenges and solution ideas (strongly encouraged to have at least one novel/strong component)
	 - Evaluation strategy (initial results are welcome to include)
